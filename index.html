About Us

Empowering individuals

The JP Foundation International is a not-for-profit organization founded in 2020. We are committed to raising global giants, contributing significantly to knowledge and improving the quality of lives around the world.

We focus on enhancing quality education, good health and well-being as well as striving to reduce poverty among our global community.
